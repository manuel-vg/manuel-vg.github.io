Delivered lectures for first-cycle studies during the summer semesters (since the 2021/22 academic year) as part of the Cognitive Science program, coordinated by the Faculty of Philosophy, University of Warsaw.

### Introduction to Deep Learning for Natural Language Processing
- Undergraduate course introducing neural networks and their application to NLP. Covers core models (MLPs, CNNs, RNNs, and Transformers), evaluation metrics, and vector space representations. Includes hands-on training using Python, TensorFlow, and PyTorch for tasks such as text classification and sentiment analysis.
